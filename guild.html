📖 About Regenesis

From wanderers to warriors, scholars to healers; Regenesis is where every path through the Jianghu finds a home. We are a guild reborn from the belief that strength comes not from one discipline, but from all of them united. Whether you seek glory in battle, mastery of the world's secrets, or simply a place to belong; your next chapter starts here.

🎯 Mission Statement

Regenesis exists to build a thriving community for all players of Where Winds Meet. Competitive fighters, veteran explorers, master creators, and casual wanderers all have a home here. We grow through shared effort, mutual respect, and the understanding that every member, no matter their playstyle, makes the guild stronger.

⚡ Guild Pillars

No Gatekeeping
New players are welcomed and mentored, not ignored or left behind.
Play Your Way
PvE, PvP, exploring, social, roleplay — all playstyles are valued equally.
Collective Growth
Weekly quotas are met together. No one person carries the load alone.
Earn Your Rank
Progression is contribution based, not seniority-based.
Real Community
Discord encouraged, social, and genuinely invested in each other.

🗡️ Divisions

Members can belong to more than one division. These are not rigid factions; they exist so people know who to call on for what content.

The Blades
PvP-focused members, GvG roster, and dueling culture.
The Vanguards
PvE crew for dungeons, talents, collectibles and co-op content.
The Wayfarers
Explorers, builders, fashionistas and those walking the casual path.
The Untethered
"Bound to no discipline. Ready for all of them." Players who move fluidly between content. Relaxed when the moment calls for it, fully locked in when it matters.

👑 Ranks

Rank Title Role Overview
1 Guild Master Guild founder and final voice on all decisions.
2 Co-Founder Core founding members who helped establish the guild's direction.
3 Officer Trusted leaders managing day-to-day operations and member support. Dedicated officers in charge of each Division.
4 Member Active members who have passed the trial period and contribute regularly.
5 Apprentice New recruits in their first two weeks, finding their footing.
6 Friend of the Guild Non-members/Alts with limited Discord access. Friendly players and associates.

📜 Guild Rules

  • Contribute weekly. Guild Events/Activities are a must, shows your commitment to the guild and our community, helps us keep track of members that still play the game or may need help from fellow guildies with their tasks. Members who go inactive without notice will be removed to keep the guild healthy.
  • Respect all playstyles. Do not pressure PvE players into PvP or casuals into hardcore content. Every path is valid here. While we approach PvP with a competitive and disciplined mindset, we respect those that do not wish to part take.
  • No toxicity. Friendly banter is welcome. Harassment, ridicule of new players, and poor sportsmanship are not.
  • Communicate. Going offline for a while? Let an Officer or the Guild Master know so you are not removed by mistake.
  • Help sustain the guild. The Guild Base and our progression live on member contributions. Every activity point/fund counts.
